What is Smart Money?

In summary, the Smart money meaning refers to the funds or capital controlled by investment
professionals who are more experienced and well-informed than retail investors.
Expert financial entities or individuals who can foresee market trends
and get the most out of the profits manage the capital.
Banks and funds are two examples of such financial entities.

Break of structure

The break of structure is a concept that focuses on identifying shifts in the market's overall trend.
A break of structure is when the price sets a new high or new low while breaking the previous one.
Often referred to as BOS, it is the valuable tool in navigating the markets effectively using Smart Money Concepts
It helps you as a trader to gain a clear view of market direction and identify high probability opportunities,
along with the execution strategy from our mentorship programme.

Order block

This is one of the crucial concepts to understanding SMC.
Essentially, order blocks refer to to a market condition when central banks, and large financial institutions accumulate or distribute large quantities of an asset through several big orders.
An order block is a price area where banks have placed large orders, either to buy or sell a particular asset and their sheer size often causes swings in the market. They're also referred to as supply and demand zones.
When price taps into an order block we expect a large volume move, however there should still be conflunce with other concepts to confirm the setup.


High time frame order block


Order blocks are much more significant on higher time frames
Always perform a top-down analysis where you look at the higher time frames first, to identify
key order blocks, and then go down to a lower timeframe when the price enters the order block range
to refine your trade entry.

Liquidity

This is a very important concept to understand when trading these Smart Money setups.
Liquidity gives us an indication of where price will run through with strong impulsive candles.
It allows us to anticipate large moves into these areas and set take profit targets at these levels.
The definition of liquidy is 'the availability of liquid assets to a market or company'
Liquidity is a measure of the ease of ability to enter and exit in a market at the desired price based on the number of buyers and sellers in that market.
Where there are areas of liquidity, the price will move quickly in and out of these areas to fill their orders, meaning price will not stay there very long.
This is represented by large candle sticks and bodies around these liquidity levels.
There are different forms of liquidity and a way to use liquidity to our advantage is through liquidity grab, which is covered in details in our mentorship programme.
